Burger Sauce

Ingredients

  1. Mayonnaise
  2. Ketchup
  3. Mustard
  4. Garlic powder
  5. Onion powder
  6. Hot sauce
  7. Lemon or vinegar for acidity

Steps

  1. Add mayonnaise.
  2. Add half as much ketchup as mayonnaise.
  3. Add quarter as much mustard as ketchup.
  4. Taste and add more mayo for creaminess, ketchup for sweetness and mustard for bitterness.
  5. Add garlic and onion powder to taste.
  6. Add hot sauce to taste.
  7. Adjust acidity to preference with lemon juice or vinegar.
