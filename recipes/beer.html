Beer

A simple guide to making your own beer. See bottom of the page for a real recipe.

Ingredients

  • Malt extract
  • Yeast
  • Hops
  • Water

    • In a real beer making scenario you would require some more ingredients. See bottom of page for real recipe.

Steps

  1. Sanitize the equipment.
  2. Mix the ingredients.
  3. Wait.
  4. Add the fermented liquid into bottles and cap the bottles.
  5. Wait.
  6. Your beer is ready.(Not actually. There are a lot more that need to be done. Please find a real recipe.)
